So... Who are Khruangbin?

Khruangbin are a three-piece band hailing from Texas, US. The band is made up of Laura Lee on bass, Mark Speer on guitar, and Donald Ray "DJ" Johnson Jr. on drums.

The band is known for blending global musical influences, such as classic soul, dub and pyschedelia. Particularly their first album's influences draws from the history of Thai music in the 1960s, whilst their second album has influences from Spain and the Middle East. Personally I only discovered their music in the last year and I liked their unique sound and influences from music both past and present.
